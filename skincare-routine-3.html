Key Rules & Tips
Avoid conflicts, maximize results
⏱️ Your New Routine Philosophy
Weekdays = 4 steps only (cleanse, essence, moisturize, SPF). Weekends = full luxury treatment. A simple routine you actually do beats a perfect one you skip!
⭐ Retinal Saturday + Retinol Sunday
This back-to-back weekend power duo is your secret weapon for the 11 line. Retinal (Saturday) is stronger — it converts to retinoic acid faster. Retinol (Sunday) reinforces the effect. Never swap the order or use both on the same night.
💆 Targeting Your 11 Line
Apply your retinal eye treatment and peptide serums directly between the brows — not just under the eyes. Use your ring finger and press gently rather than rubbing. Consistency every weekend will show results in 6–8 weeks.
🛢️ Double Cleanse Rule
Never use two oil cleansers back to back. Medicube Oil Cleanser and Feel Daily Hemo Oil are both first-cleanse options — alternate them. Always follow with a water-based second cleanse.
☀️ SPF Every Single Day
Even on your 5-minute weekday routine — SPF is the one step that cannot be skipped. It prevents the 11 line from deepening and protects all your weekend active work.
💧 Oils Always Go Last
Olehenriksen Hydrabarrier and Byroe Peony Tea Oil are always the very last step on weekend nights — they seal everything in. Never apply oil before your actives or moisturizer.
⚠️ Never Mix Actives
BHA (Fri), Retinal (Sat), Retinol (Sun) each get their own night. Never combine them. If your skin feels irritated, drop back to just retinol on Sunday and give retinal every other Saturday.
🧴 Benched Products — Rotate In
Great products waiting their turn: AHA serums (Lactic Acid, Glycolic Acid, Saturday Skin), Azelaic Acid, extra toners and serums. Rotate these in on weekends when you want to switch things up.
